PHOTOGRAPHY CLUB

About Us

Welcome to the Photography Club of MIT WPU University, located in Pune, Maharashtra, India. This club is a haven for photography enthusiasts who love to capture the beauty of the world through their lens. We organize workshops, photo walks, and competitions to help members refine their skills and express their creativity. Whether you're an experienced photographer or a beginner, the Photography Club offers an inclusive space to learn, share, and grow.

Achievements

  • First place in the National Photography Competition (2023).
  • Featured in the "Lens Masters" Photography Exhibition (2022).
  • Organized a campus-wide photo contest with over 500 participants.
  • Conducted workshops with renowned photographers, including Dabboo Ratnani.
