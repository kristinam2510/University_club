MUSIC CLUB

About Us

Welcome to the official Music Club of MIT WPU University, located in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Our club brings together students passionate about music, offering a platform for collaboration, learning, and performance. From classical Indian tunes to modern genres, we explore all musical styles. We regularly organize concerts, jam sessions, and workshops, enriching the vibrant campus life. The Music Club is not only a hub for artists but also a space for everyone to discover and nurture their musical talents.

Achievements

  • Second place in the Pune City Intercollegiate Music Festival (2023).
  • Performed at the Maharashtra State Cultural Fest (2022).
  • Collaborated with the Indian classical maestro Pandit Hariprasad Chaurasia.
  • Organized over 30 successful music workshops and events on campus.
