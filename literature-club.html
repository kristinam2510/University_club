LITERATURE CLUB

About Us

Welcome to the official Literature Club of MIT WPU University, located in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Our club is a vibrant space for those who love to read, write, and discuss the world of literature. From classic novels to contemporary poetry, we celebrate diverse genres and literary styles. The Literature Club offers a platform for members to explore their creative writing, engage in discussions, and organize events like book readings, writing workshops, and author interactions. Whether you're an avid reader, aspiring writer, or someone passionate about storytelling, this is the place to share your love for literature.

Achievements

  • Winner of the Intercollegiate Literary Fest (2023) in Creative Writing.
  • Organized the annual Book Fair and Author Meet-up event on campus.
  • Collaborated with renowned authors for interactive workshops and talks.
  • Published a collection of short stories and poems written by club members.
