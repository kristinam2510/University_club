DEBATE CLUB

About Us

Welcome to the official Debate Club of MIT WPU University, located in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Our club is a dynamic and engaging platform for students who love to speak, argue, and explore important topics. We foster a culture of respectful debate, critical thinking, and intellectual discussion. Whether you're an experienced debater or just starting, we provide opportunities to participate in debates, public speaking events, and skill-building workshops. The Debate Club hosts regular inter-college debates, participates in national competitions, and encourages members to develop their public speaking, argumentation, and leadership skills. Join us and become part of a club that sharpens minds and amplifies voices!

Achievements

  • Winner of the National Intercollegiate Debate Championship (2023).
  • Represented MIT WPU at the World Debate Tournament in Singapore (2022).
  • Organized the Annual Debate Fest with over 20 colleges participating.
  • Hosted workshops on public speaking, argument construction, and critical thinking for students across the university.
