ROBOTICS CLUB

About Us

Welcome to the official Robotics Club of MIT WPU University, located in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Our club is dedicated to exploring the exciting world of robotics and artificial intelligence. We provide a collaborative environment for students interested in engineering, programming, and innovation. From building robots for competitions to hands-on workshops, our club offers members the opportunity to learn, experiment, and create cutting-edge technology. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced enthusiast, the Robotics Club is the place to bring your ideas to life.

Achievements

  • First place in the National Robotics Competition (2023).
  • Developed an AI-powered robotic arm for industrial applications.
  • Participated in the International RoboFest (2022) held in Dubai.
  • Organized over 20 workshops on robotics, AI, and automation for students.
